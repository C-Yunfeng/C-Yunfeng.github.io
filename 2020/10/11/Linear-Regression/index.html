一、损失函数及求导：

1.表达方式一：

假定： $$x{(i)}:n*1$$，$$\theta:n*1$$，$$y{(i)}:1*1$$；$$h_\theta(x{(i)})=\thetaTx^{(i)}$$

  • 损失函数J(θ)J(\theta)J(θ)：

J(θ)=12m∑i=1m(hθ(x(i))−y(i))2=12m∑i=1m(θTx(i)−y(i))2\begin{aligned} J(\theta)&=\frac{1}{2m}\sum_{i=1}^m(h_\theta(x^{(i)})-y^{(i)})^2\\ &=\frac{1}{2m}\sum_{i=1}^m(\theta^Tx^{(i)}-y^{(i)})^2 \end{aligned} J(θ)​=2m1​i=1∑m​(hθ​(x(i))−y(i))2=2m1​i=1∑m​(θTx(i)−y(i))2​

  • J(θ)J(\theta)J(θ)求导：

∇θJ(θ)=∂J(θ)∂θ=1m∑i=1m(hθ(x(i))−y(i))xj(i)\begin{aligned} \nabla_\theta J(\theta)&=\frac{\partial J(\theta)}{\partial\theta}\\ &=\frac{1}{m}\sum_{i=1}^m(h_\theta(x^{(i)})-y^{(i)})x_j^{(i)} \end{aligned} ∇θ​J(θ)​=∂θ∂J(θ)​=m1​i=1∑m​(hθ​(x(i))−y(i))xj(i)​​

2.表达方式二：

假定：$$X:mn、b:n1、y:m*1；h_b(X)=Xb$$

则损失函数J(θ)J(\theta)J(θ)如下：

J(b)=(Xb−y)2=(Xb−y)T(Xb−y)=(bTXT−yT)(Xb−y)=bTXTXb−bTXTy−yTXb+yTy\begin{aligned} J(b)&=(Xb-y)^2\\ &=(Xb-y)^T(Xb-y)\\ &=(b^TX^T-y^T)(Xb-y)\\ &=b^TX^TXb-b^TX^Ty-y^TXb+y^Ty \end{aligned} J(b)​=(Xb−y)2=(Xb−y)T(Xb−y)=(bTXT−yT)(Xb−y)=bTXTXb−bTXTy−yTXb+yTy​

求导：

\begin{equation*} \begin{split} \nabla_b J(b) &= \nabla_b (b^T X^T Xb - b^T X^T y - y^T Xb + y^T y)\\\\ &= \nabla_b (b^T X^T Xb) - \nabla_b (b^T X^T y) - \nabla_b (y^T Xb) + \nabla_b (y^T y)\\\\ &= \nabla_b (b^T (X^T X)b) - \nabla_b (b^T (X^T y)) - \nabla_b ((y^T X)b) + 0\\\\ &= (X^T X + (X^T X)^T)b - X^T y - (y^T X)^T\\\\ &= 2 X^T X b - 2 X^T y\\\\ &= 2 X^T (Xb-y) \end{split} \end{equation*}

其中，常用的矩阵求导公式：

∂(AX)∂X=AT\frac{\partial(AX)}{\partial X}=A^T∂X∂(AX)​=AT，∂(AX)∂XT=A\frac{\partial(AX)}{\partial X^T}=A∂XT∂(AX)​=A，∂(XTAX)∂X=AX+ATX\frac{\partial(X^TAX)}{\partial X}=AX+A^TX∂X∂(XTAX)​=AX+ATX，∂(XTa)∂X=a\frac{\partial(X^Ta)}{\partial X}=a∂X∂(XTa)​=a

3.损失函数代码(Octave)
%损失函数
function J = computeCost(X, y, theta)
m = length(y); % number of training examples
h=X*theta;
srqt=(h-y).^2;
J=1/(2*m)*sum(srqt);
end

二、梯度下降求解(Gradient Descent)

1.迭代

Repeat:{θj:=θj−α1m∑i=1m(hθ(x(i))−y(i))xj(i)}Repeat:\{\\ θ_j:=θ_j-α\frac{1}{m}\sum_{i=1}^{m}(h_θ(x^{(i)})-y^{(i)})x_j^{(i)}\\ \} Repeat:{θj​:=θj​−αm1​i=1∑m​(hθ​(x(i))−y(i))xj(i)​}

2.Octave代码
%梯度下降
function [theta, J_history] = gradientDescent(X, y, theta, alpha, num_iters)
m = length(y); % number of training examples
J_history = zeros(num_iters, 1);
for iter = 1:num_iters
    h = X * theta;
    j = h - y;
    sums = X' * j;
    delta = 1 / m * sums; 
    theta = theta - alpha * delta;
    J_history(iter) = computeCost(X, y, theta);
end

五、评价指标

  • 评价标准：
    Linear Regression常用决策系数(coefficient of determination)：
    R2=SSRSST=1−SSESSTR^2=\frac{SSR}{SST}=1-\frac{SSE}{SST}R2=SSTSSR​=1−SSTSSE​，其中：
    SST(Sum of Squares for Total)=$$\sum_{i=1}{m}(y_i-\overline{y_i})2$$，表示数据的总方差；
    SSR(Sum of Squares for Regression)= ∑i=1m(yi^−yi‾)2\sum_{i=1}^{m}(\hat{y_i}-\overline{y_i})^2∑i=1m​(yi​^​−yi​​)2，表示已被模型（/自变量）解释的方差；
    SSE(Sum of squares for Error)=∑i=1m(yi^−yi)2\sum_{i=1}^{m}(\hat{y_i}-y_i)^2∑i=1m​(yi​^​−yi​)2，未被模型解释的方差（/成本）；

因为线性回归的损失函数为：

J(θ)=12m∑i=1m(hθ(x(i))−y(i))2=12m∑i=1m(y^−y)2\begin{aligned}J(θ)&=\frac{1}{2m}\sum_{i=1}^{m}( h_θ({x^{(i)}} )-y^{(i)})^2\\ &=\frac{1}{2m}\sum_{i=1}^{m}(\hat{y}-y)^2 \end{aligned}J(θ)​=2m1​i=1∑m​(hθ​(x(i))−y(i))2=2m1​i=1∑m​(y^​−y)2​

所以当最小化J(θ)J(\theta)J(θ)时，有∑iy=∑iy^\sum_{i}y=\sum_{i}\hat{y}∑i​y=∑i​y^​，由此可进一步得到：

∑i(y^−y‾)2+∑i(y−y^)2=∑i(y−y‾)2\sum_{i}(\hat{y}-\overline{y})^2+\sum_{i}(y-\hat{y})^2=\sum_{i}(y-\overline{y})^2 i∑​(y^​−y​)2+i∑​(y−y^​)2=i∑​(y−y​)2

SSR+SSE=SSTSSR+SSE=SST SSR+SSE=SST

R2取值为[0,1]R^2取值为[0,1]R2取值为[0,1]，越接近1，拟合程度越好，R2=1R^2=1R2=1时表示回归方程可以完全解释因变量的变化；而如果R2R^2R2值过低，则说明自变量和因变量之间可能不存在线性关系。
