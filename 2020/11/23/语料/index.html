  • 摘录方法
  1. 该个人品质有助于实现人哪方面发展
  2. 该社会文化有助于实现社会哪方面和谐进步
  3. 该科技设备有助于实现人怎样的便利，社会怎样的进步
  4. 或者该科技设备不利于实现人哪方面发展，不利于社会怎样的进步
  5. 环境保护有助于实现社会怎样发展
  6. 或者环境污染不利于社会哪方面发展

该品质/行为 lays the foundation for us to set ambitious goals, overcome formidable obstacles and then reap the happiness of success.
翻译：该品质/行为 为我们打下了设定远大目标，克服艰难障碍，并收获成功快乐的基石。
适用于哪些个人品质/行为：乐观，自信，坚持，合作，爱心，感恩等等等。（显然，掌握了这个写作角度，许多个人类话题作文都能被套用）

    • “Economist” 即可表示书名

3. 环境保护
3.1 句子

  • We can improve the quality of air we breathe everyday by taking some concrete steps to advance the Green City movement.【提升空气质量_措施】

  • There is no arguing that smog is a problem that we can no longer ignore.【重视雾霾_叙述】

  • 【例子】We can make a difference collectively by doing simple things in our daily life, such as making environmental friendly choice while shopping and opting public transportation when traveling.【从小事做起_措施】

  • Smog is not built in a day and cannot be attributed to a single factor.【雾霾_叙述】

  • So the process to get rid of it would also take time and require persistent efforts from both the public and the government.【措施】

  • Long-term traffic and pollution reductions would depend on educating the public to use public transport more, and on government's using public money to construct and run efficient system.【政府】

5. 通用词语
5.1 插入语

  • 副词插入语：frankly, especially, fortunately, indeed, however

  • 短语插入语：generally speaking, to tell the truth, in a sense, in a word, strange to say

  • 短句插入语：I suppose, as I see it, I believe, what is important

  • 万能插入语：rather than肯前否后，这个短语可以放在句子中的任何一个成分后面。

    【例句】It abolishes the presumption of innocence and places the citizen at the service of the state rather than the other way round.

5.2 两个功能句
